The Problem

Tailoring is the biggest lever — and the biggest time sink

A tailored résumé is how you stand out against hundreds of applicants. But it costs 30–60 minutes each.

So every job seeker faces the same trade-off:

Apply broadly

Blast a generic résumé → filtered out by ATS and recruiters.

Apply selectively

Tailor a few by hand → miss most opportunities.

…and that's after the daily grind of scanning job boards just to find the right roles.

The Solution

Two specialist agents, one orchestrator, three human gates

Agent 1 — Job Finder

Searches LinkedIn + Indeed + Glassdoor, scores each role against your résumé, returns a ranked shortlist. Never pads.

Agent 2 — Resume Customizer

Asks a few batched clarifying questions, then tailors your résumé to each approved job from your real experience.

🧑 Gate 1 approve the shortlist  ·  🧑 Gate 2 answer gaps with real facts  ·  🧑 Gate 3 you apply

How It Works

Onboard once → search → approve → clarify → tailor → delivered

1 · Search

Agent 1 pulls fresh roles (Google for Jobs), filters on level & sponsorship, scores & ranks.

2 · Approve

You pick which to pursue. Only those continue.

3 · Clarify

One batched question set fills gaps with real answers.

4 · Tailor

Agent 2 writes a one-page résumé (.docx + PDF) per job.

5 · Deliver

Auto-emails you the packet + apply links; logs to a Google Sheet.

6 · Apply

You click apply. Job Lander never submits for you.

The Differentiators, Up Close

Three things competitors don't do

Anti-fabrication as a feature

When a JD needs something your résumé doesn't show, Agent 2 asks — batched, once — instead of inventing. Gaps you don't close are flagged honestly, never papered over.

Real, compliant job search

Reaches LinkedIn-sourced roles via Google for Jobs (SerpApi) — full descriptions, no scraping, no ToS violation, no risk to your accounts.

Human-in-the-loop by design

Three gates keep judgment with you: where to apply, what's true about you, and the irreversible decision to apply.

Generalized & free

Swap one config for any seeker. Self-hosted Google MCP + free SerpApi tier — no paid automation platform.
